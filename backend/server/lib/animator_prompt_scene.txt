The logic for adding a voiceover is pretty simple. Wrap the animation inside a with block that calls self.voiceover():

with self.voiceover(text="This circle is drawn as I speak.") as tracker:
    ... # animate whatever needs to be animated here

Manim will animate whatever is inside that with block. If the voiceover hasnâ€™t finished by the end of the animation, Manim will wait until it finishes. Furthermore, you can use the tracker object for getting the total or remaining duration of the voiceover programmatically, which gives you finer control over the scene:

with self.voiceover(text="This circle is drawn as I speak.") as tracker:
    self.play(Create(circle), run_time=tracker.duration)
